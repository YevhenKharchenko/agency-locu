About the Agency

The Mission Behind LOCU

Agency LOCU is a fictional intelligence division brought to life through real-world games. We design high-stakes escape rooms, mobile spy puzzles, and corporate team experiences inspired by covert operations. Every scenario puts players into the shoes of agents navigating encrypted intel, physical clues, and psychological challenges. Whether it’s for a night out or a company event, we blend creativity with espionage to create unforgettable tactical adventures.

Physical Escape Rooms

Operate from the Shadows

Our physical escape rooms drop you directly into thrilling, cinematic scenarios: defuse the bomb, escape enemy detention, or recover stolen files from a double agent. Every detail—from props to soundscapes—is designed for maximum immersion. Difficulty levels range from beginner to expert, with rotating missions and time-sensitive events.
